FinBox Term Loans empower your customers to fund their personal or business capital requirements. Term loans facilitate your customers long term objectives. Repayment period range from 12 to 60 months. 
## About the Term Loan
* Loan amount up to 5 lakhs
* Loan tenure up to 60 months
* Instant approval
* Completely digital loan application
* Loan amount is disbursed in 24-48 hours
* Collateral-Free loans
* Transparent loan application process with no hidden fees
* Multiple Repayment Modes
* Prompt customer support
* Flexible repayment - Customers can pay the EMI before the due date.

## User Journey

### Part 1 - Apply for the term loan

1. **Term loan banner** - The user clicks the banner which initialises the SDK powered by FinBox. 
  
2. **User gives the consent to share data** - User gives the consent to share the credit history, device data ([Device Connect](/product/products/device-connect)), bank statements ([Bank Connect](/product/products/bank-connect)), GST (if applicable)

3. **Complete the e-KYC** Following this the user completes his eKYC by submitting his
    * PAN Card
    * Address proof (Aadhaar or Passport)
    * Selfie
    * Business registration proof.

4. **Verify the Bank Details and Setup NACH**

   * **Bank details verification** - User enters the bank account details for the verification of account details.
 
   * **Setup e-NACH**- User sets up the e-NACH for auto debit of EMI

5. **Completes the application process** - User digitally signs the loan agreement and completes the application journey.

### Part 2 - Loan  amount disbursal

Loan amount gets credited to the user account. 

### Part 3 - EMI Repayment
**Payment Reminder** - We remind the customer to pay the emi through app notifications and SMS.

**Repayment** - User can choose to pay the EMI through the app or NACH is presented for the emi deduction.
  
## Key Features

* Can pay the EMI before the due date.
* Tenure and repayment is decided by the lender
* KYC is done within seconds
