## About Embedded Finance
Embedded Finance Infrastructure natively enables credit for all merchants within a B2B E-Commerce platform. It handles the end-to-end lending flow, including the customer journey, loan offer generation, lender partnerships, and third-party integrations, repayment etc.

## Why Embedded Finance Works?


* ***In App Experience*** Embeddable in-app experience

* ***Fully Managed*** Fully managed credit line lifecycle embedded into your app. Including application, withdrawal, and repayment.

* *** Tailored Credit Products***
Customized credit products that suit the exact needs of the customer provided at the exact time of need work much better than one-size-fits-all products that digital lenders provide. Embedded Finance enables the provision of flexible, tailored, and in-context credit.

### Increased approval rates by leveraging platform data

Embedded Finance combines lending expertise, alternative data underwriting, and data from the platform to credit score merchants and approve more customers.

* *** Instant approval and activation*** Credit Line sanctioned immediately through a fully digital process. All withdrawals are also instant.


* *** Offer a line up to ₹5 Lakhs*** Credit line up to Rs 5 Lakhs at lowest-in-market interest rates.

* *** Flexible repayment options*** Bullet repayment or monthly installment based repayment options with flexible tenure

* ***Helpful for NTC customers*** Can give loans to NTC(New to Credit) customers by underwriting the alternate data.

* *** Encourages up-selling and cross-selling*** Enabling credit gives platforms access to merchants for both up-selling and cross-selling.
