## What is FinBox?

FinBox is a credit infrastructure and risk intelligence company. We build various products in the digital lending value chain. 
We enable

 - Any company to launch a digital credit program on their platform by leveraging our end-to-end credit infrastructure.
 - Lenders to improve their approval rates and collection percentage by providing risk intelligence

## Why FinBox?


We thrive at the intersection of cutting-edge technology and deep credit expertise

### Deep business domain expertise
FinBox clubs together technological prowess with deep lending expertise. Our intelligent AI/ML engines make processes such as KYC, credit risk assessment, underwriting significantly faster, streamlined and effective.
### Custom-built for success
We help you stem delinquencies, while increasing the number of loans disbursed. We bring with us industry- leading lenders to support credit offerings and help you drive higher customer satisfaction and bump up sales
### 16 Million Strong dataset of borrowers
16M borrower dataset enables precise conceptualization of credit products for various populations. We assess more than 1M applications every month and approve 50% more applicants than traditional lenders.
